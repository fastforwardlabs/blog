Serverless data science
Cloud computing has transformed enterprise IT. It makes it possible to rapidly scale up and down a complex and global infrastructure, without the overhead of a datacenter. But living in the cloud can be expensive, and you still need to maintain computers and their operating systems, even if they are virtual. That’s why we’ve been watching with interest the rise of “serverless” computing. Serverless has the potential to open up “big data” work to mere mortal data scientists who don’t have the budget or engineering support for a long-lived analytics cluster, and to make life simpler and reduce costs for those that do.
